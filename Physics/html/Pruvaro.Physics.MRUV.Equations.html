Torricelli's equation

One of the most well-known non-trivial results in constant acceleration systems is the Torricelli's equation, which relates velocities and positions without making explicit mention to time.

Theorem torricelli_equation (m : MRUV.model) :
  let: @MRUV.Pack x0 v0 a x v xAxm vAxm := m in
  let Δx : R -> R := fun t => (x t) - x0 in
  forall (t : R), (v t) ^ 2 = v0 ^ 2 + 2 * a * (Δx t).
Proof.
  case: m => x0 v0 a x v xAxm vAxm Δx.
  unfold MRUV.position_axiom in xAxm. unfold MRUV.velocity_axiom in vAxm.
  unfold Δx.
  move => t. rewrite vAxm xAxm. lra.
Qed.
